    Make Your Own Time Mchine

    If your interest is in generating a Time Machine for ADHydro, you may download the TMAPS code from the CI-WATER GitHub repository. After following the prerequisites for installation in the Readme.md, users can modify the 'tmaps_adhydro.py' for the specific ADHYdro files being processed.
